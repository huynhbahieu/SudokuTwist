Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Sudoku Twist (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Sudoku Twist mobile application.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media stored on your device
  • Precise location

The game does not require account creation and does not store personal data on our servers.

2. Information We Collect Automatically

The app may collect limited non-personal data for the purpose of improving user experience, app performance, and advertising.

2.1. Analytics (Anonymous)

We may use analytics providers (such as Firebase Analytics or similar) to collect:

  • Device type
  • App version
  • Game progress (levels played, completion time)
  • Crash logs

This data is anonymous and cannot identify you.

2.2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Sudoku Twist uses Google AdMob to display banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Anonymous device identifiers
  • Approximate location (for ad relevance)
  • Ad interaction data

AdMob may use advertising IDs (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your system settings.

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads

3. In-App Purchases (IAP)

The app includes optional in-app purchases such as:

  • Remove Ads
  • Coin Packs

Payments are processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not have access to your credit card information at any time.

4. Children’s Privacy

Sudoku Twist is suitable for general audiences but not intended for children under 13 without parental supervision.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data Storage

The app stores limited game progress locally on your device:

  • Daily streak
  • Game statistics
  • Purchased items (Remove Ads)
  • Theme selections

This information never leaves your device unless transmitted anonymously for analytics or advertising purposes as listed above.

6. Third-Party Services Used

Sudoku Twist uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – Ads
  • StoreKit / Google Play Billing – In-app purchases
  • Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics (optional) – App performance

Please review their privacy policies:

  • AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Anonymous usage data may be shared with service providers solely to operate and improve the app.

8. User Rights

You may:

  • Turn off personalized ads
  • Reset your advertising identifier
  • Clear app data or uninstall the app to remove local data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
